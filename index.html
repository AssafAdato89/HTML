ABOUT ME

My name is Assaf Adato, I am 32 years old, living in Tel-Aviv, Israel. For the last 7 years I have been involved in nightlife - I have owned the Alphabet Club, Mila Bar, and the Drama - which is a unique complex that combines a restaurant, bar, cocktails, courtyard and nightclub. My biggest love is for music, and for the last 5 years I am a professional producer and DJ of house and techno music . I played in all the most prestigious clubs and events in Tel Aviv and Israel, and also played around the world: Ibiza, Poland, Austria and Sri Lanka. And in addition, I also produced some original tracks, some of which I even released on reputable labels in Europe.
